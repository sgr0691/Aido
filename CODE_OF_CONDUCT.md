# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to or uses Aido, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:
- Being respectful and professional
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Providing constructive feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the project and community
- Showing empathy towards others
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes

### Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, etc.)
- Public representation of the project
- Communication about the project in public forums

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers may take action including:
- Warning the offender
- Temporary ban from project spaces
- Permanent ban for repeated or serious violations
